## Ingredients

- Plain flour: 500g (4 cups)
- Baking powder: 30g (3 heaped tsp)
- Salt: 1 pinch
- Sugar: 80g (⅓ cup + 1 tbsp)
- Butter: 100g (1 stick)
- Eggs: 2
- Buttermilk: 200ml (¾ cup)
- Raisins: 150g (1 cup)

Yields about 11 scones.

1. Prepare eggwash - 1 egg yolk mixed with a pinch of salt and sugar
1. Mix flour, the baking powder with the butter
   (you can freeze the butter first to make it easier to grate)
   until you get a crumbly texture.
1. Add a pinch of salt, the sugar, the fruit..
1. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and buttermilk.
1. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix
   until just combined. Do not develop gluten.
1. Dump the dough on a floured surface and roughly stack the
   dough on itself. Do not develop gluten.
1. Flatten the dough to a thickness of about 3cm.
1. Use a 7cm round ring to cut out the scones.
1. Place the scones on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
1. Brush the tops with the eggwash.
