---
title: Balsamic-port reduction
tags: ["sauce", "reduction", "balsamic", "port", "vinegar", "dessert"]
---

- 1/3 cup of balsamic vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp red port wine

1. Add everything to pot.
1. Bring to a simmer, medium heat.
1. Stir and reduce by half (5 minutes)
1. Let cool and store. No need to refregirate.

Serve on vanilla ice cream.
