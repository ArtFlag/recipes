---
title: Poulet au vinaigre
tags: ["french", "chicken", "dinner", "vinegar"]
---

- Chicken thighs: 6
- Shallots: 2
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Chicken broth: 1 cup
- White wine: 1 cup
- Red wine vinegar: 1/3 cup
- Tomato paste: 1 tbsp
- Tarragon
- Butter: 1 tbps


1. Brown thighs skin down until golden.
1. Set chicken aside, keep some fat in the pan and brown shallots and garlic
1. Add chicken broth and white wine and red wine.
1. Bring to a simmer.
1. Scrape fond, reduce by half, bring chicken back. skin up above the liquid.
1. Put pan in oven at 180c.
1. When chicken at 90c (35 minutes ish), take out from oven.
1. Remove chicken from pan.
1. Add 1 tbsp tomato paste bring to simmer.
1. Add taragon and a knob of cold butter.
1. Add a splash of red vinegar and serve.
