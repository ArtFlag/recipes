---
title: Roasted Potatoes
tags: ["potato", "roast", "technique"]
---

- Potatoes (Russet for fluffiest interior, Yukon Gold for creamier): 1kg
- Fat (duck fat, rendered chicken fat, or avocado oil): 3–4 tbsp
- Salt

1. **Parboil:** start in cold salted water, bring to boil, cook for 5-6 minutes.
1. **(Optional) Baking soda:** add a pinch to the boiling water. Alkaline environment breaks down surface pectin, creating a rough starchy exterior. More texture = more crunch.
1. **Rough up:** drain, return to pot, shake vigorously until edges are ragged and fluffy.
1. **Dry thoroughly:** moisture kills crispiness.
1. **Coat in thin layer offat:** toss well so every rough surface is coated.
1. **Don't crowd:** single layer with space between pieces. Crowding = steaming.
1. **No lining:** roast directly on metal. Parchment or foil traps steam.
1. Roast at 200 °C until golden and crisp (40+ minutes).
1. Season
