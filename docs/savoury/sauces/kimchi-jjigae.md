title: Kimchi jjigae

## Ingredients

- 120-200 g pork belly, sliced
- water: 380ml (1.5 cups) or anchovy/chicken stock
- sliced very fermented kimchi: 150g
- garlic, grated: 30g (3 cloves)
- spring onions: 30g (2 stalks)
- onion, sliced: 100g (1 small)
- gochujang: 1 tbsp
- chilli powder (gochugaru): 1-2 tsp / 30g
- soy sauce: 1 tsp
- fish sauce: 1 tbsp
- tofu, soft: 100g
- sesame oil: 1 tsp

## Instructions

1. Heat a pot over medium heat and add the pork belly. Cook until lightly browned.
1. Add the kimchi and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add the gochugaru, and soy sauce. Mix well.
1. Pour in the water or anchovy/chicken stock and bring to a boil.
1. Reduce the heat to a simmer and add the tofu and onion.
1. Cook for 15-20 minutes until the flavors meld together.
1. Drizzle with sesame oil and garnish with green onions before serving.
