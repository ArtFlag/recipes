- 2 oz irish whiskey
- 1/4 ounce amaretto
- 1/8 teaspoon bitters

1. Mix ingredients in a mixing glass with ice.
1. Stir for 60 seconds.
1. Strain into an old fashioned glass with ice.
1. Express orange peel over glass and garnish with it.
