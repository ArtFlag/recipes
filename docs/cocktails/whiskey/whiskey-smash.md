---
title: Whiskey smash
tags:
  - bourbon
  - mint
  - citrus
  - shaken
  - crushed-ice
---

- Lemon: 1/2 a lemon
- Simple syrup: 1/2 oz
- Whiskey: 2 oz
- Mint leaves: 6-7 leaves

1. Cut half a lemon in 4 wedges, and muddle them in the shaker.
1. Add Whiskey, syrup, mint leaves.
1. Shake with ice.
1. Double strain in glass.
