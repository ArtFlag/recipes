- 2 ounces vodka (or gin, tequila, whisky, cognac, pisco...)
- 3/4 lime juice
- 4-5 ounces ginger beer

1. Fill in cup with ice cubes.
1. Pour vodka and lime and top with ginger beer.
1. 2 dashes of bitters
