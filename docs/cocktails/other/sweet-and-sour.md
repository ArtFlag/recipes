title: Sweet and sour mix
tags:

---

- 1 part sugar
- 1 part water
- 1 part lime juice
- 2 parts lemon juice

1. Juice the limes and lemons.
1. Heat citrus juice, water and sugar just until it dissolves.
1. Cool to room temp.

Store refrigerated for up to 1 week.
