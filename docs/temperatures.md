## Beef, lamb, venison, chops, roasts

- **Medium rare**: 54-57
- Medium: 57-63
- Medium well: 63-68
- Well done: 68+

## Pork, veal, fresh hams

- Medium rare: 54-57
- **Medium**: 57-63
- Medium well: 63-68
- Well done: 68+

## Chicken/turkey, ground or whole

74c
